Mt. Province,
Sagada

Sagada is a charming and picturesque town located in the northern part of the Philippines, specifically in the Mountain Province on the island of Luzon. Known for its stunning natural beauty, rich culture, and adventurous activities, Sagada has become a popular destination for both local and international travelers seeking a unique and immersive experience.

The town is nestled within the Cordillera Central mountain range, surrounded by breathtaking landscapes of lush green valleys, terraced rice paddies, and majestic limestone cliffs. Its high altitude, approximately 1,500 meters (4,900 feet) above sea level, provides a cooler climate compared to the lowlands, making it a pleasant escape from the tropical heat.
