Mt. Province,
Sagada

Sagada is a charming and picturesque town located in the northern part of the Philippines, specifically in the Mountain Province on the island of Luzon. Known for its stunning natural beauty, rich culture, and adventurous activities, Sagada has become a popular destination for both local and international travelers seeking a unique and immersive experience.

The town is nestled within the Cordillera Central mountain range, surrounded by breathtaking landscapes of lush green valleys, terraced rice paddies, and majestic limestone cliffs. Its high altitude, approximately 1,500 meters (4,900 feet) above sea level, provides a cooler climate compared to the lowlands, making it a pleasant escape from the tropical heat.

Discover the Enchanting Marlboro Hills: A Journey to Serenity in Sagada

Marlboro Hill

Perched high on the Cordillera Mountain Range, Marlboro Hills offers breathtaking panoramic views of the surrounding valleys, terraces, and mountains. Visitors are captivated by the sea of rolling hills, lush greenery, and a unique sea of clouds that often envelop the area during early mornings.

The Marlboro Hills is a favorite spot for nature lovers, hikers, and adventure seekers. Tourists can embark on various trekking trails that lead to the viewpoint, providing an opportunity to witness the stunning vistas up close. The hike is well worth the effort, as it rewards travelers with a sense of tranquility and a chance to reconnect with nature's beauty.
